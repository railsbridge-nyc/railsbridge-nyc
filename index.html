RailsBridge works to increase diversity in tech by putting on awesome free events.

We hold free 1.5 day Workshops in the New York City area to help women learn how to program their first websites in Ruby and Ruby on Rails.

Get Involved

Heard of RailsBridge and want to get involved? Great!

We have an active and passionate group of volunteers and teachers who want to help you run the next RailsBridge in NYC. Join our Google Group and introduce yourself and what you'd like to contribute.
